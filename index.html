How to Play

Objective: Protect your Miami house from increasingly severe hurricanes from 2025 to 2100!

Goal: Keep all 5 essential systems functional throughout the 75-year timeline:

  • 🛏️ Bedroom (2nd Floor)
  • 🚿 Bathroom (1st Floor)
  • 🍳 Kitchen (1st Floor)
  • ⚡ Electrical System (Ground)
  • ❄️ Cooling System (Ground)

Game Progression:

  • ⛅ Normal Days: Rest and recovery periods - systems recover naturally
  • 🌀 Hurricane Days: Intense storms with flooding and wind
  • 🌊 Hurricane frequency and intensity increase every decade
  • 💨 Wind speeds increase over time (up to 150+ mph by 2100)
  • 📈 Sea level gradually rises throughout the game

Mechanics:

  • Drag cards to deploy defenses
  • Each card costs "Budget Points" and regenerates over time
  • Defenses only fail on HURRICANE DAYS (safer to build on normal days)
  • Budget regenerates faster on normal days (3x faster)
  • Higher floors are safer from flooding
