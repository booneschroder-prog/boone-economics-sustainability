How to Play

Objective: Protect your Miami house from increasingly severe hurricanes (Cat 1-5) from 2025 to 2100!

Win Condition: Keep these 4 systems functional:

  • 🛏️ Bedroom (2nd Floor)
  • 🚿 Bathroom (2nd Floor)
  • ⚡ Electrical System (Attic)
  • ❄️ Cooling System (Attic)

Defense Cards (Max 3 active between hurricanes):

  • Dikes/Levees: 2 elixir/ft. Build barriers. Permanent.
  • Elevate Structure: 15 elixir/ft. Raises house. Permanent.
  • Backup Generator: 6 elixir. Protects electrical. 4-day downtime if fails.
  • Aerodynamic Roof: 5 elixir. Strengthens roof. 2-day downtime if fails.
  • Solar PV Panels: 9 elixir. 75% failure rate. Synergizes with generator. 5-day downtime if fails.
  • Hurricane Room: 13 elixir. Safe room. Flood-proof. Permanent.

Game Mechanics:

  • ⛅ Normal Weeks: Budget regenerates slowly. Plan your defenses carefully.
  • 🌀 Hurricane Weeks: Each hurricane has a name and category (1-5). Defenses can fail! Pay repair costs after.
  • 💨 Higher categories more likely over time
  • 🌊 Sea level rises 5 feet by 2100
  • 💰 Repair costs scale with hurricane strength and damage
  • 📰 Read climate news headlines for immersion
