How to Play

Objective: Protect your Miami house from increasingly severe hurricanes (Cat 1-5) from 2025 to 2100!

Win Condition: Keep these 4 systems functional:

  • 🛏️ Bedroom (2nd Floor)
  • 🚿 Bathroom (2nd Floor)
  • ⚡ Electrical System (Attic)
  • ❄️ Cooling System (Attic)

Defense Cards:

  • Dikes/Levees: 5 elixir/ft (1-10 ft). Build barriers. Permanent. 50% failure below, 100% above height.
  • Elevate Structure: 15 elixir/ft. Raises house. Permanent. (Construction downtime in weeks)
  • Backup Generator: 6 elixir. Protects electrical. 4-week downtime if fails.
  • Aerodynamic Roof: 5 elixir. Strengthens roof. 2-week downtime if fails.
  • Solar PV Panels: 9 elixir. 75% failure rate. Synergizes with generator. 5-week downtime if fails.
  • Hurricane Room: 13 elixir. Safe room. Flood-proof. Permanent.

Game Mechanics:

  • ⛅ Normal Weeks: Budget regenerates. Defenses are under construction during downtime.
  • 🌀 Hurricane Weeks: Longer duration, slower game speed. Defenses activate after downtime.
  • 💨 Higher categories more likely over time
  • 🌊 Sea level rises 5 feet by 2100
  • 📰 News headlines stack and evolve with ingame year
